About Me

April 5, 2024

My name is Liam Tyler. I’ve been doing graphics for about 7 years. In terms of work, I was previously a senior rendering engineer at Deviation Games working on an unnanounced UE5 game. Before that, I was a rendering engineer at Sledgehammer Games working on the Call of Duty games. I’m currently taking a small sabbatical from work to focus on my health and to hike a bunch of mountains in Colorado this summer.

This blog is mostly going to be a combination of various graphics or coding things that I’ve found interesting, as well as learnings from working on my little hobby engine.
